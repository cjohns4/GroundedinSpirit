Hey, It’s China. Come On In.

This space is for the mind, body, and spirit. I’m a writer, a spiritual guide, a creative technologist, an educator, and an artist of everyday beauty. I build websites by hand, braid hair with intention, interpret dreams, design learning experiences, and write with depth and clarity.

Born in Harlem in the 80s—a time of fire and resilience—I’ve always carried the gift. From the womb, I’ve seen beyond the veil, moved between planes, and listened to the quiet truths most people overlook. That knowing shapes everything I create.

With degrees in Learning, Design & Technology, Computer Science, and Computer Information Systems, I bring both technical fluency and emotional intelligence to my work. But this site isn’t just about credentials—it’s about connection, story, and spirit.

Here you’ll find my projects, my poetry, my offerings, and my reflections. Whether you’re seeking a spiritual reading, curious about learning new tech, or just drawn to the energy—welcome. You’re in the right place.
